MORTAL KOMBAT

Mortal Kombat is a 2021 American martial arts fantasy film directed by Simon McQuoid (in his feature directorial debut) and written by Greg Russo and Dave Callaham, based on the video game franchise of the same name created by Ed Boon and John Tobias. It is the seventh installment in the Mortal Kombat film series and a reboot of the franchise. The film stars Lewis Tan, Jessica McNamee, Josh Lawson, Tadanobu Asano, Mehcad Brooks, Ludi Lin, Chin Han, Joe Taslim, Hiroyuki Sanada, and Josh Helman.
